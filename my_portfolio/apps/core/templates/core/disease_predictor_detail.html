Multi-Species Disease Detection

AI-powered disease detection for Plants, Humans, and Animals. An offline-first, privacy-focused desktop application built with PyTorch and PySide6.

Python PySide6 PyTorch MIT License

Key Features

  • ✅ Multi-Species Support: Detect diseases in plants, humans, and animals from one application.
  • ✅ Offline-First: Works completely offline, no internet required after setup.
  • ✅ Privacy-Focused: All data stays on your device.
  • ✅ Research Integration: Automatic Wikipedia and PubMed research fetching.

Core Capabilities

Powered by a custom-trained MobilenetV2 model validated on 26 disease classes.

AI Chatbot

Interactive guidance for disease info.

Report Gen

Create detailed PDF & HTML reports.

Smart Validation

Rejects invalid images automatically.

Geo Mapping

Visualize disease locations.

How It Works

Image Upload → Validation → Preprocessing → AI Inference → Results ↓ ↓ ↓ ↓ ↓ User Input Reject Invalid Resize/Norm MobileNetV2 Display (diagrams/text) Transform Prediction + Research
